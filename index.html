Recipe for Disaster

First, warm the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Cook the garlic in the oil until it’s lightly golden and softened.
It doesn’t take long so keep an eye on it and lower the heat if it’s browning too fast.
Reserve a tablespoon of the starchy pasta water before you drain the pasta.
Add the cooked spaghetti, along with the tablespoon of starchy pasta water,
to the skillt and toss it with the garlic oil.
Add the chopped parsley and Parmesan cheese, toss it all again and serve!
